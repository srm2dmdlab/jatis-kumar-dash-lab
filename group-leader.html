Group Leader

Dr. Jatis Kumar Dash

Associate Professor, Department of Physics, SRM University-AP

JKD

Dr. Jatis Kumar Dash is an Associate Professor in the Department of Physics at SRM University-AP, Andhra Pradesh, India. He earned his Ph.D. from the Institute of Physics (IOP), Bhubaneswar, under the Homi Bhabha National Institute (HBNI), Department of Atomic Energy, Government of India. He completed two postdoctoral appointments abroad — first at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI), New York, USA (2012–2015), and then at Yonsei University, Seoul, South Korea (2016–2017), where he was awarded the Korea Research Fellowship by the National Research Foundation of Korea.

His research sits in experimental condensed matter physics, centered on the fabrication of large-area 2D layered materials — transition metal dichalcogenides, transition metal oxides, and MXenes — and their heterostructures, aimed at device applications including solar cells, field-effect transistors, photodetectors, and sensors.
